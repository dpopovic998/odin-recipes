Cevapi

Description

Cevapi is traditional dish from the Balkans, most often served with flatbread, onion or kajmak

There are serveral types of cevapi, depending on the aread they come from

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place onion in hot water
  2. Put beef in water
  3. Add rest of ingredients
